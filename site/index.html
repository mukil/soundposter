What the **** is soundposter? 

a soundposter is an arbitray sequence of sound (-streams) arranged ontop of your personal graphic-file, accessible as an audible website.

soundposter` is a platform which has the potential to empower people to start cinematic thinking in a browser.

 

For whom is soundposter made? 

soundposter was created to act reanimating for the genre of classical music graphic (e.g. artworks, booklets) in times of digital music distribution. therewith it`s aim is to support musicians, publishers as well as organizers of various music events to innovatively promote/paste their sound all over the web.
