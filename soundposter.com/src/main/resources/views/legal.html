Legal

At this early stage of soundposter.com, please just get in touch with us if you would like to use soundposter.com as your platform, and we will help you do it.

So, if you notice any irresponsible publishing behaviour on soundposter.com please do not hesitate to report this incident to abuse|@|soundposter.com.

Further clarification: We do not host or stream any music from our web-servers. Instead, technically spoken, we provide an alternative and user-curated access to online-music which was published elsewhere and where the availability of the stream is under direct control by the artist or some rightfulness publisher.

If you have informed concerns on the rightfulness of certain contents on our website, please get in touch with us directly via phone (see below), we assure you that we'll investigate your issue and try to resolve it.

Privacy & Analytics

Informations concerning your privacy on soundposter.com: For analytical purposes we collect anonymized (e.g. 192.168.x.x) usage statistics for all visits to our platform in (a) the access and error logs of our webservers and (b) in a Piwik installation based on one of our webserver. Irregularly we're creating anonymized usage reports and then delete the old data from our webservers. We do not share these anonymized access-data with anyone else, except that we do make anonymized usage-statistics available to the creators of a soundposter.
