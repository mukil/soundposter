What is it and what can I do with it?

Soundposter is a new format for audible websites which "brings back the face to digital audio distribution" (jri). On soundposter.com you can create audible websites to promote your festival, event or upcoming gig or you can orchestrate your favourite set of sounds available on the web with images.

Money, Honey?

We will provide soundposter completely free of ads. The unfavorable aspect of that: As a consequence it is not free of charge. But then the good news again: It's low priced. On this platform will offer distinct subscription possibilities for all creators of soundposters as well as the option to publish single soundposter.
