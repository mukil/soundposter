Idea background & Credits

The name soundposter stands for an interaction format and with this platform we aim to revitalize music-graphics (namely covers, booklets, gig-Poster, ...) by giving the creation process into the hands of our users. The name was synthesized after more than 2 years of supportive, friendly and open talk with many really cool people. Below you can find more about these guys.

The complete soundposter.com platform was designed & developed with some concentration and my own two hands (mikromedia.de). But what I want to point here is: I am standing on the shoulders of giants who are working hard to keep the web open and you can find out about all of them in the technical section of our blog.
