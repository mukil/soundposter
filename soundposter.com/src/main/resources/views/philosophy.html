What is this and what can I do with it?

With soundposter.com one can visualize any playlist on the web across many devices. Soundposter names an interaction format for audible websites and your looking at its publishing platform.

With this platform we aim to re-animate music-graphics for the digital music ecosystem by giving the creation process of visual setlists, lp-covers, booklets and gig-posters into the hands of listeners and artists alike.

Or as jri has put it: “Soundposter brings back the face into digital music.” Have a look at what others did with soundposter in our /browse section.

Money, Honey?

Our business plan is to provide soundposter.com completely free of ads. As a consequence of that: The usage of the soundposter-tool for publishers will not be completely free of charge. The good news is: It will be low priced.

Additionally we will offer distinct subscription possibilities for all creators of soundposters as well as the option for everyone to publish single soundposters.

Custom service

If you wish to publish your festival guide audible, filterable and accessible to all people on the world wide web endorsing smartphones, and have it visually attractive and embeddable for other websites (like TSF did), do not hesitate to leave us a message and we'll get right back to you.

Furthermore, soundposter claims to follow the rules of fairness and to communicate openly. So, if you have any kind of questions, stimulations or ideas for enhancements we are looking forward to reading your input or concerns about our work at info|at|soundposter.com or via Twitter.
