What is it and what can I do with it?

Soundposter is a new format for audible websites which "brings back the face to digital audio distribution", as jri put it.

And with this platform you can create awesome audible websites to promote your festival or event, like an upcoming gig. Or you can easily orchestrate your favourite set of sound-streams available on the web to images and publish them wherever you want.

Have a look at what others did with soundposter in our /browse section.

Money, Honey?

We plan to provide soundposter completely free of ads and as a consequence the usage for publishers will not be completely free of charge. The good news are: It's low priced and we will offer distinct subscription possibilities for all creators of soundposters as well as the option for everyone to publish single soundposters.

Additionally we'll have on offer some unique features like publishing your festival guide audible, filterable and accessible to all your visitors endorsing smartphones.
